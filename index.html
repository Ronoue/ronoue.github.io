About Me

I am a dedicated researcher and academic in the field of Robotics and Mechatronics Engineering. With a strong foundation in Bio-mechatronics, Machine Learning, embedded systems, wearable devices, and IoT applications, I am passionate about developing innovative solutions that address real-world challenges.

Currently pursuing my Master's degree at the University of Dhaka, while working as a part-time research assistant at MAIM Lab, University of Dhaka, I focus on biomedical signal processing and machine learning applications in healthcare. Projects

IoT-enabled Scalable Closed Hydroponics System with Smart Parameter Control

Undergraduate Thesis Project

Jan 2023 - Jan 2024

  • Designed and implemented an IoT-based scalable closed hydroponics system.
  • Monitored and controlled physical parameters required for plant growth (pH, Electrical Conductivity, CO2, Humidity, Air Temperature, Water Temperature).
  • Utilized Arduino Nano and Arduino Mega as nodes to handle sensor and actuator data separately.
  • Developed a web-based dashboard for real-time monitoring with data visualizations and live video feed.
  • The proposed system led to a 78% to 288% increase in fresh mass yield, compared with two control groups: one using the conventional soil-based technique while the other used the Deep Water Culture (DWC) technique.
  • Project was fully funded by the ICT Division under the ICT Innovation Fund.
  • Currently under the first round of reviews at Smart Agricultural Technology.

2 DOF, Single Claw Crawler Bot using Q-learning

Topic: Reinforcement Learning
